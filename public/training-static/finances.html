05 / Fiscal Computation

The Ethiopian Income Tax Engine

The system calculates the client's **Ethiopian Business Income Tax** dynamically. These formulas are baked into the Eloquent model (`MonthlyLedger.php`) to ensure perfect compliance.

Official Corporate Tax Law

For Category A/B corporate taxpayers in Ethiopia, business income tax is assessed at 30% - 35% of Net Income Before Tax (NIBT).

Code Formula: Net Profit > 0 ➔ Tax = (Net Profit * 0.35) - 24,600 ETB
